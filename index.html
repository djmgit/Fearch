Fearch

Search FTP servers over the web for music, videos, eBooks and other content

developed and maintained by NIT-Dgp OS

How to use?

Its too easy!!

Open Fearch and enter name of a song, Video or book that you want to search for, in the search field.
Select a category from the given options, that is, Music, Video or Books. You can also mention a file type in the search field itself after the search item using a period. For eg: You can enter Highway to Hell.mp3.Click on Enter
Voila! You will be presented with a list of FTP server links which contains items related to your search.You may visit any suitable link and view your required item.

Yup! It is that easy!!
